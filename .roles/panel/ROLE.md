# Panel Reviewers

Six permanent voices that review every CERES artifact — specs, plans,
catalog entries, playbook files, and the pitch narrative. They represent
the core productive tensions of local-production economics.

## Selection Rule

All six review every artifact. No exceptions. The tensions between them
are the entire point — an artifact that satisfies all six has survived
genuine scrutiny from every worldview that matters for CERES.

## Scoring Responsibility

Panel reviewers provide **qualitative** feedback, not numeric scores.
Numeric scoring against `scoring/RUBRIC.md` is a separate step. The
panel's job is to say what works, what doesn't, and why — in their own
voice, with the intellectual discipline of their tradition.

## Review Output Format

```markdown
## P-{N} — {Name} — Review of {artifact-id}

**Overall:** {1–2 sentence verdict}

**What works:**
- {Specific praise with line / section / field reference}

**What doesn't:**
- {Specific critique with line / section / field reference}

**The question I'd push on:**
{The one thing this reviewer most wants the author to address}
```
