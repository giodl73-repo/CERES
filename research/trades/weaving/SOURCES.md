# Weaving — Consolidated Bibliography and Placeholder Audit

## Purpose

This document consolidates every citation across the seven Plan H deliverables for the weaving trade (Tasks 1–7). It serves two functions. First, it is the bibliography: all named sources grouped by subject area, with full citations, one-line usage notes, and confidence tiers. Second, it is the author's verification queue: every `[CITATION-NEEDED: ...]` placeholder across the seven documents is listed in the Placeholder Audit table below, with a suggested research lead and urgency ranking. Items blocking Plan H catalog consumption are flagged explicitly. No new citations are introduced in this document.

The weaving corpus is substantially larger than the smithing corpus — 7 documents versus 7 documents, but roughly double the placeholder count — owing to the trade's geographic, fiber-type, and organizational complexity across the four anchor cultures. The spinner-to-loom ratio, humidity range, and throughput figures are the three highest-priority unverified parameters because they feed directly into `sim_params` fields across all Plan H weaving catalog entries.
