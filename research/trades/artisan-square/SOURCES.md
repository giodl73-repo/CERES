# Artisan Square — Consolidated Bibliography and Placeholder Audit

## Purpose

This document consolidates every citation across the seven Plan J research deliverables
for the artisan-square trade (Tasks 3–9). It serves two functions. First, it is the
bibliography: all named sources grouped by subject area, with full citations, one-line
usage notes, and confidence tiers. Second, it is the author's verification queue: every
`[CITATION-NEEDED: ...]` placeholder across the seven documents is listed in the
Placeholder Audit table below, with a suggested research lead and urgency ranking.
Items blocking Plan K catalog authoring are flagged explicitly. No new citations are
introduced here.

Unlike the smithing SOURCES.md (which drew on medieval economic history journals and
published academic works), the artisan-square bibliography is primarily contemporary:
building codes, franchise disclosures, program guides from operating facilities, and
occupational health regulations. These sources are more verifiable in principle — most
are available via public websites or public regulatory filings — but model knowledge
of specific current figures (rates, studio counts, FDD Item 19 data) may not be
current. The citation policy of `[CITATION-NEEDED]` over fabrication applies with
particular force here: financial figures cited as benchmarks for Plan K catalog entry
economics must be sourced before those entries can be promoted past draft status.
