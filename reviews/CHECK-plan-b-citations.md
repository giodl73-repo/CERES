## Suspicious / Fabricated Citations (must fix)

**None.** No citations were classified SUSPICIOUS or FABRICATED.

The two PARTIAL findings are minor bibliographic errors on confirmed real works. Both should be corrected before final publication:

### PARTIAL #1 — Needham 1958, vol. IV (Song China chapter)

- **Location:** `research/cultures/song-china/smithing.md`, inline citations in "Period and Geography" and "The Forge" sections; Sources list entry #3
- **Claim supported:** Vol. IV cited for (a) early Chinese coal/iron transition predating Europe, (b) hydraulic bellows in Northern Song ironworks
- **Problem:** Year 1958 is wrong. Volume IV did not begin publication until 1962. The relevant content (hydraulic bellows, iron/steel metallurgy) is in Vol. IV Part 2, published 1965.
- **Recommended fix:** Update all instances of `Needham 1958, vol. IV` to `Needham 1965, vol. IV, pt. 2` and correct the Sources list entry to:
  > Needham, Joseph. 1965. *Science and Civilisation in China*, vol. IV, pt. 2: *Physics and Physical Technology — Mechanical Engineering*. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press.
- **Risk level:** Low integrity risk (the series is genuine and the content is correctly assigned to the correct volume series); medium bibliographic risk (any reader following the 1958 citation will find Vol. III, not the intended work).

### PARTIAL #2 — Wallace 1978 subtitle error (American Frontier chapter)

- **Location:** `research/cultures/american-frontier/smithing.md`, Sources list entry #2
- **Claim supported:** Structural reference for household labor and supply-chain dependencies in early American industrial context
- **Problem:** Subtitle given as "The Growth of an American Village in the Early Industrial Age." Correct subtitle: "The Growth of an American Village in the Early Industrial Revolution."
- **Recommended fix:** Update Sources list entry #2 subtitle from "Early Industrial Age" to "Early Industrial Revolution."
- **Risk level:** Minimal. One-word substitution; no reader would be unable to locate the book.
